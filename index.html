About Me

My name is Jinliang Zhang. I grew up in China, and moved to Canada in 2017 for graduate study. Therefore, I'm proficient in both Chinese and English. I completed my master program at University of Western Ontario in 2018.

I majored in Electrical and Eletronic Engineering during my undergraduate study.

During my graduate study, I became passionate about software building and programing. I have provided several demos of my projects in this website for your reference.
